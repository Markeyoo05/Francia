French Riviera (Côte d'Azur):

The French Riviera is known for its glamorous beaches, luxurious resorts, and crystal-clear waters. Popular beaches along the French Riviera include:

  • Plage de Pampelonne in Saint-Tropez
  • Plage de la Croisette in Cannes
  • Paloma Beach in Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat
  • Plage de Nice in Nice

    • Corsica

    This Mediterranean island boasts stunning beaches with pristine waters and rugged landscapes. Some notable beaches in Corsica include:

  • Palombaggia Beach near Porto-Vecchio
  • Rondinara Beach near Bonifacio
  • Giulia Beach near Porto-Vecchio
  • aleccia Beach accessible by boat or hiking trail

    • Normandy

    The Normandy region on the northern coast of France offers picturesque beaches with dramatic cliffs and historic significance. Notable beaches in Normandy include:

  • Omaha Beach, famous for its role in the D-Day landings during World War II
  • Deauville Beach, known for its elegant promenade and beachfront villas
  • Étretat Beach, with its iconic natural arches and cliffs

    • Brittany

    Brittany is known for its rugged coastline, charming villages, and sandy beaches. Popular beaches in Brittany include:

  • Plage de l'Écluse in Dinard
  • Plage de la Baule in La Baule-Escoublac
  • Plage de Morgat in Crozon
  • Quiberon Peninsula beaches

    • Basque Country

    The Basque Country, located in southwestern France, offers picturesque beaches with a unique cultural vibe. Some notable beaches in the Basque Country include:

  • Grande Plage in Biarritz
  • Hendaye Beach near the Spanish border
  • Plage de la Côte des Basques in Biarritz
  • Socoa Beach in Saint-Jean-de-Luz
